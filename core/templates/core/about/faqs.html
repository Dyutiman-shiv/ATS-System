Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do I need a different version of resume for each job role I am applying?

No, not always. You don’t have to change your resume every time you apply to a job, especially if the jobs you’re applying, are similar.

In what format do I receive my completed resume?

The resume can be edited and customised on the platform and is then downloadable in the PDF format.

Will I be notified for all the job openings?

No, you will be notified on the job openings based on your profile only. You can also choose to unsubscribe from the notifications, in case you have already found a job.

How will a verified resume help me?

A verified resume creates a greater impact and certainly adds credibility to your profile. Candidates have seen significantly improved conversions with a verified resumes.
